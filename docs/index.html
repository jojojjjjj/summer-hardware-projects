关于课程

这是一个为高中生设计的暑期硬件实践课程。12–15 天，从第一次焊接到最后一次答辩，亲手打造 9 个真实开源硬件项目。不只是夏令营，是真正的工程训练。

A summer hardware practicum for high school students. In 12–15 days, from your first solder joint to your final defense, you will build 9 real open-source hardware projects hands-on. Not just a summer camp — real engineering training.
