关于课程

这是一个为高中生设计的暑期硬件实践课程。12–15 天，从第一次焊接到最后一次答辩，亲手打造 9 个真实开源硬件项目。不只是夏令营，是真正的工程训练。

A summer hardware practicum for high school students. In 12–15 days, from your first solder joint to your final defense, you will build 9 real open-source hardware projects hands-on. Not just a summer camp — real engineering training.

联系我们
9+真实项目社区验证的开源硬件项目改编，非玩具示例
5完整工程大学级课程体系：提案、评分、Git 工作流
∞社区支持活跃开源社区 + 论坛资源，遇到问题不孤立

课程阶段

01
项目调研与选型Research & SelectionDay 1–3

研究开源项目，评估可行性，选择最适合的技术方案与硬件平台

Research open-source projects, evaluate feasibility, and select the optimal technical approach and hardware platform

02
硬件搭建与调试Hardware BuildDay 4–6

元器件选型、电路搭建、接线调试，让硬件跑起来

Component selection, circuit assembly, and wiring debug — get the hardware running

03
软件开发与集成Software DevDay 7–9

固件编写、传感器集成、模块调试，让系统联动起来

Firmware coding, sensor integration, and module debug — make the system work as a whole

04
系统优化与完善System OptimizeDay 10–12

性能调优、边界测试、细节打磨，让产品稳定可靠

Performance tuning, edge-case testing, and polish — make the product stable and reliable

05
文档撰写与展示Docs & DemoDay 13–15

技术文档、最终演示、项目答辩，完成从做到讲的全闭环

Technical documentation, final demo, and project defense — close the loop from doing to presenting
